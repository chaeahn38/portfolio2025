Introduction
Chae Ahn (b. 1996) is a graphic designer and type design student from Seoul, South Korea, currently based in Lausanne. Her work explores the relationship between letterforms, identity, and storytelling through editorial design, visual identity systems, and experimental type. She has collaborated with artists, institutions, and publishing collectives such as Ningen Paper Press — with whom she has organized talk sessions, led workshops, and participated in book fairs. In 2025, she began her MA in Type Design at ECAL to develop multilingual, research-driven typefaces — from book typography to motion and web environments.
